# Privacy Policy for Provisions

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026

Provisions ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile app for recipe management, shopping lists, pantry inventory, and meal planning.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
We collect information you provide directly in the app, including:
- account information such as email address and name
- household information, including household members you add or invite
- recipes, pantry items, shopping lists, meal plans, and other content you create in the app
- messages or information you send when contacting support

### Information collected automatically
We may collect limited technical and usage information automatically, including:
- device information
- operating system
- app version
- crash data
- app usage and analytics data

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- provide and operate Provisions
- save and sync your app data
- support household and collaboration features
- improve app performance, reliability, and usability
- understand how features are used
- respond to support requests
- protect against abuse and misuse

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use **Firebase** to support app functionality. Firebase may process data for services such as authentication, cloud storage/database, analytics, and crash reporting, depending on which Firebase features are enabled in the app.

Firebase is provided by Google. You can learn more about how Google handles data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app, including infrastructure, analytics, authentication, database, and crash reporting providers.

We may also disclose information if required by law or as necessary to protect our rights, users, or the public.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app and operate the service, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
